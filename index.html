About Us

AI RAMIL & AL BAHAR OFFICE EQUIP. is a leading provider of Original and Remanufactured Laser toner, Ribbons, Label Cassettes and ink cartridges founded in 2011 in response to the growing need for superior quality cartridges at competitive prices. Over the years, our ability to accelerate new product development,prototype and improve time-to-market has made us the chosen partner to many of our customers. As a result our customers are better positioned to take care of competition as they have more time to concentrate on business strategies and also have a flawless product to market.
Our cartridges are manufactured from production site that has facilities which incorporate the latest processing equipment, making it one of the most effective and flexible manufacturing centers. The continuous quality improvement systems enshrined by the "Total Quality" philosophy are reflected in the company's recognition of the importance of Quality leading to the achievement of SGS Certification, ISO 9001:2008 Certification and also the Environmental Management Systems Certificate ISO 14001:2004. The production facility has also acquired the Imaging Council Technology's endorsement of the STMC which is the seal of approval to let the end-user know that the laser cartridges have been tested to stringent standards.

Our aim is to provide you with a comprehensive and wide range of original and Remanufactured cartridges for top brands like HP, Brother, Canon, Samsung and Xerox. Our 100% Quality guarantee can give you the peace of mind knowing that you will always be satisfied and any issue will be dealt with by our expert customer service team who are just a phone call away. With growing delighted customers across Europe, we are dedicated to providing you with the ink and toner cartridges that you need to grow your business.
